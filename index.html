About the Document

What is a Certificate of Grades?

A Certificate of Grades (COG) is an official document issued by OLScho's Registrar's Office that lists all your subject grades per semester or academic year.

It is duly signed by the Registrar and bears the school seal, making it a verified and authentic record of your academic performance.

Common uses include applications for scholarship, employment, graduate school, government examinations, and school transfers.

Process

How to Request Your COG

Simple steps to get your Certificate of Grades from OLScho's Registrar's Office.

01
📝
Fill Out Request Form

Accomplish the COG Request Form available at the Registrar's Office or download it from the OLScho portal.

02
📁
Submit Requirements

Submit the accomplished form together with your valid school ID and any other required supporting documents.

03
💳
Pay the Processing Fee

Proceed to the Cashier's Office to pay the applicable fee. Keep your official receipt (OR) as proof of payment.

04
📦
Claim Your COG

Return to the Registrar's Office on the scheduled release date. Present your OR and valid ID to claim your document.

Requirements

What You Need to Submit

Ensure you have all the required documents ready before requesting to avoid delays.

🎓 For Currently Enrolled Students
  • Duly accomplished COG Request Form
  • Valid OLScho School ID (current school year)
  • Official Receipt of payment from the Cashier
  • No existing academic or financial hold or balance
  • Authorization letter (if claiming through a representative)
🏅 For Alumni / Graduates
  • Accomplished COG Request Form (downloadable online)
  • Any valid government-issued ID with photo
  • Official Receipt of payment from the Cashier
  • Photocopy of your student clearance or graduation certificate
  • Authorization letter + representative's valid ID (if applicable)
